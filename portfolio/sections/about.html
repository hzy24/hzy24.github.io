About Me

I am thrilled to join the first cohort of Max Planck Artificial Intelligence Network (MP-AIX) fellows, heading to Max-Planck-Institut für Kernphysik (MPIK) and Heidelberg University to pursue my Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Jim Hinton and Prof. Dr. Mathias Niepert.


I am broadly interested in data-driven astrophysics, with a particular taste for using data-driven methods to study interesting issues across the field. My work so far has involved multiple areas, including cosmology, high-energy astrophysics, exoplanets, and radio astronomy data processing.


Alongside like-minded friends, I co-founded a fun club called [astrocademix](https://astrocademix.github.io/). Here, we aim to explore intriguing astrophysical questions at the intersection of all kinds of fascinating, cutting-edge methods!
